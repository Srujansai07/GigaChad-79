### Battle System

**Challenge Friends:**
- 7-day discipline battles
- Loser posts "I lost to [winner] in a discipline battle" on their story
- Group challenges: "Our friend group vs theirs"

**The "Demon" Boss Battle:**
- Every day, a "Demon" appears (e.g., "The Soyboy," "The Matrix Agent")
- Deals damage to whole squad every hour
- Users deal damage by completing "Deep Work" blocks
- If squad fails to kill boss in 24 hours, everyone loses HP

**Permadeath:**
- If user's HP hits 0, account level RESETS
- Lose streak, rank, and "Creds"
- Drives the "75 Hard" mentality‚Äîno "try again" without cost
